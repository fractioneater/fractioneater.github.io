Miscellaneous

I also like to write—a poem of mine has been published in the Imprint, the HHS literary magazine. I'm too self-conscious to publish most of my writing, but there are a couple I'm comfortable sharing: Humorous Piece and Third Time's the Charm.

Occasionally I compose and arrange music. I only publish some of my pieces, but those will be on my MuseScore profile.

Graphic design is another passion of mine. I am one of the lead designers of the Imprint because I was one of the few people who became comfortable with Adobe InDesign quickly in my Creative Writing class. I prefer to do everything in Figma, but web frontend is okay... in Svelte. I would completely redo this website in SvelteKit if I knew it wouldn't take too long.
